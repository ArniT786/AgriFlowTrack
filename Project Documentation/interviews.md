# Interviews

## Objective

The purpose of conducting interviews was to understand the practical challenges faced by stakeholders in agricultural inventory management and identify system requirements.

---

## Participants

| Participant   | Role                 |
| ------------- | -------------------- |
| Participant 1 | Farmer               |
| Participant 2 | Farmer               |
| Participant 3 | Processor            |
| Participant 4 | Warehouse Manager    |
| Participant 5 | Agricultural Officer |

---

## Interview Questions

### General Questions

1. What challenges do you face in managing agricultural products?
2. How do you currently store production records?
3. How do you track inventory levels?
4. How do you monitor perishable products?
5. What information would help improve your operations?

---

## Interview Findings

### Farmers

#### Common Challenges

* Difficulty estimating seed requirements
* Poor record management
* Lack of storage information
* Limited inventory visibility

#### Requirements

* Crop tracking
* Harvest records
* Storage recommendations
* Inventory dashboard

---

### Processors

#### Common Challenges

* Tracking production dates
* Managing packaging information
* Monitoring batch details

#### Requirements

* Batch management
* Packaging records
* Production tracking

---

### Warehouse Managers

#### Common Challenges

* Product expiration tracking
* Temperature monitoring
* Inventory control

#### Requirements

* FEFO management
* Shelf-life monitoring
* Warehouse reporting

---

### Agricultural Officer

#### Recommendations

* Centralized database
* Data analytics
* Inventory reporting
* Digital traceability

---

## Summary of Requirements

| Requirement            | Frequency Mentioned |
| ---------------------- | ------------------- |
| Inventory Tracking     | High                |
| Shelf-Life Monitoring  | High                |
| Temperature Monitoring | High                |
| Batch Management       | Medium              |
| Reporting              | Medium              |
| Data Analytics         | Medium              |

---

## Conclusion

Interview results indicate that stakeholders require a centralized digital platform capable of managing inventory, monitoring storage conditions, tracking shelf life, and improving information sharing across the agricultural supply chain.
