# Product Requirement Document (PRD)

## Product Name

AgriFlowTrack

## Product Vision

To provide a centralized web-based agricultural inventory management platform that enables farmers, processors, and preservers to efficiently manage agricultural products, reduce waste, and improve supply chain visibility.

---

## Product Objective

The primary objective of AgriFlowTrack is to improve inventory management and storage efficiency for perishable and non-perishable agricultural products through centralized information management and FEFO-based tracking.

---

## Problem Statement

Agricultural stakeholders currently depend on fragmented information sources for production, storage, packaging, and inventory management. This leads to:

* Poor inventory visibility
* Crop spoilage
* Inefficient storage management
* Lack of traceability
* Increased operational costs
